Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi, Jharkhand, is a celebrated Indian cricketer renowned for his exceptional leadership and wicket-keeping skills. Known as "Captain Cool," he has led India to numerous victories, including all three ICC trophies. Dhoni is one of the most prolific and successful captains in cricket history.

Personal Achievements

  • Full Name: Mahendra Singh Dhoni
  • Born: 7 July 1981, Ranchi, Jharkhand, India
  • Spouse: Sakshi Dhoni
  • Height: 5 ft 9 in (175 cm)
  • Nickname(s): Mahi, Thala, Captain Cool
  • Military Rank: Honorary Lieutenant Colonel, Indian Army

Career Highlights

  • Captain of India (2007–2017 in limited overs, 2008–2014 in Tests).
  • Led India to victory in the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, 2011 Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
  • Represented Chennai Super Kings in the IPL, winning multiple championships.
  • First captain to win all ICC trophies.
